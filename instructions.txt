Pizza Dough Recipe by Jesse Steiger

300g water
1-1/2 tsp yeast
1/2 tsp sugar
300g all-purpose flour
200g pizza 00 flour
2 tsp salt

Add water, yeast and sugar to bowl and let rest for 6-8 minutes.
Add flours and salt, then knead for 10 minutes.
Separate into 2 balls of dough and place each into its own bowl coated with olive oil.
Let raise for at least an hour.
